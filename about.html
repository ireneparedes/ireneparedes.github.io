About me

I am Ph.D. Student in Andy J Green’s Lab in the Department of Wetland Ecology of the Doñana Biological Station in Seville (Spain). I am currently investigating the eutrophication process in the Doñana marshes and tributary streams, where water quality issues have received too little attention.

Studies: Bachelor in Environmental Sciences (Universidad Autónoma de Madrid, Spain) and MSc in Environmental Sciences (Wageningen University, The Netherlands).
